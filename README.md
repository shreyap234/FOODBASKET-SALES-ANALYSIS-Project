# 🛒 FoodBasket Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **FoodBasket Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence project created to analyze overall sales performance, profitability, product category trends, market-wise contribution, and geographical sales distribution.

This dashboard helps businesses understand:
- Total Sales and Profit
- Profit Percentage
- Top Product Sales
- Average Shipping Time
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Market
- Sales by Year
- Sales by City
- Sales by Country
- Order Priority Distribution

## 🎯 Objective
The main objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights for better decision-making.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI**
- **Excel / CSV Dataset**
- **Data Cleaning**
- **Data Visualization**
- **DAX Measures**
- **Map Visualization**



## 📊 Dashboard KPIs
- **Total Sales:** 12.64M
- **Total Profit:** 1.47M
- **Profit %:** 0.12
- **Top Product Sales:** 625.55K
- **Average Shipping Time:** 3.97


## 📈 Key Insights
- **Technology** category generated the highest sales contribution.
- **Asia Pacific** market recorded the highest sales among all markets.
- Sales showed a **steady yearly growth trend** from 2014 to 2017.
- **New York City** contributed the highest sales among cities.
- Most orders were placed under **Medium Order Priority**.
- Sales are widely distributed across multiple countries, indicating a strong global presence.
